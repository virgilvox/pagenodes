Design System · v1.0

PATCHBAY

The visual language for PageNodes. Color-coded patch cables, squared nodes, hard block shadows, and monospace metadata. Built to look like the thing it documents: a browser that wires modules into running flows.

Electric indigo accent Patch cable signature Space Grotesk + Spline Sans Mono Light and dark
Operating principles

One accent, used sparingly

Electric indigo carries focus, links, and the active node. Everything structural stays in warm neutrals. Signal colors are functional, not decoration.

Square corners

Radii top out at 6px on nodes, 3px on controls, 2px on chips. No soft pill shapes. The grid stays honest.

Hard offset shadows

A 4px solid block shadow in the edge color. No blur, no glow. Elements read like cut paper or physical tiles.

Monospace metadata

IDs, counts, statuses, and labels run in Spline Sans Mono, uppercase, with wide letter spacing. The interface narrates its own state.

Hierarchy through font, not weight

The jump from sans display to mono label does the heavy lifting. Size and family separate roles before bold ever does.

The cable is the brand

Color-coded bezier wires appear everywhere a connection exists. Animated on marketing, functional in the editor, decorative at rest.

06

Voice and copy

PATCHBAY writes plainly. Short sentences, concrete nouns, no decoration. These rules are absolute across marketing, docs, and UI strings.

Never
No em-dashes. No emojis. No bold or italic inside prose. No filler vocabulary like delve, leverage, seamless, robust. No antithetical constructions like not X, it's Y.
Always
Lead with the concrete capability. Say what the thing does in one breath. Let monospace metadata carry numbers and state. Use periods and commas where a dash would tempt you.
On voice
PageNodes runs in your browser. No server, no install, no account. Wire nodes together and watch the flow run.
Off voice
Leverage our robust, seamless platform to unlock powerful flows. It's not just an editor, it's a revolution.
